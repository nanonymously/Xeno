The action plan — what we actually do, by tier

Three activities run the quarter — Events, ABM + interactive/game campaigns, and Inbound — aimed differently by tier. Webinar + research is a shared awareness feeder; founder LinkedIn is optional.

Top 50 Highest value / revenue — land big, high-touch
  • Events / roundtables — intimate, founder-led, retail-CXO rooms; the main SQL driver for this tier.
  • Highly personalised ABM — 1:1 sequences, buying-committee mapped (2+ contacts), tailored to each account's context.
  • Gifting — personalised, triggered when an account goes "Hot" (12 gifts · ₹3,000 each).
  • Webinar + case studies — used as warm, credibility touches.
Next 200 Closest ICP fit — highest propensity, semi-personalised at scale
  • Segmented ABM — by category / job-title / department (targeted clusters, not 1:1).
  • Interactive / game campaigns — Squid Games-style; our best-converting creative.
  • Blanket gifting — non-personalised, where it makes sense.
  • Event invites — 2nd priority, once Top 50 is worked and the target isn't yet met.
  • Webinar + case studies — core awareness + capture.
Rest Long tail — scaled, low-touch, no paid
  • Interactive content + webinar — broad awareness; accounts self-qualify in.
  • Inbound — website forms, gated case studies, ROI calculator (runs across all tiers; highest quality per touch).

How we compete

  • Capillary — enterprise-loyalty heavyweight (analyst badges, scale, acquisitions, big events). We won't out-spend it.
  • MoEngage / CleverTap — app-first engagement; win on benchmark reports, branded communities/events, case studies, AI-agent narrative.
  • Where Xeno wins — purpose-built for retail (POS/loyalty-connected, India + GCC), proven incremental revenue (Taco Bell +69%, Bestseller +40%, 60x ROI), autonomous decisioning.
  • We copy what works for challengers — retail research, case studies, intimate events, sharp POV — and skip what we can't fund.

Webinar runs thought-leadership-led, not product-led. Founder-led LinkedIn kept optional / aspirational.

Quality leads over mass — qualify before the hand-off

An engagement becomes an MQL only if it clears two gates: Fit (Top 50/200 + a real buyer/influencer, not HR/junior) and Intent (demo/pricing/form, repeat, event/webinar attend, completed game).

High intentLow intent
High fit (Top 50/200)🔥 Hot → sales in 24h, book meetingWarm → tight ABM + event invite
Low fit (rest)Watch → light nurture; enrichDrop / suppress

Every Hot lead is handed to a named owner, actioned within 24 hours, and its status updated back — fewer, higher-quality leads, and nothing goes cold.

Alignment with Signal Touch (org plan)

Signal Touch launches mid-October. This Q3 plan is its foundation quarter — built so nothing is wasted.

  • Tiers nest: our Top 50 = their top-50 high-touch · Next 200 ⊂ their top-500 outbound · Rest ⊂ their 1,500 awareness universe.
  • Do once, use twice: the research, case studies, enriched lists, and LinkedIn/WhatsApp setup we build now are exactly what the launch needs in October.
  • Hand-off: Q3's 10 SQLs + a warmed, enriched list feed the launch's 100-meeting goal.
  • Boundary: we build the engine; we don't execute the launch here.
